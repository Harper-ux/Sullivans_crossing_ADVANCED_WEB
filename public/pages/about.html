About

Who are we?

Here at Sullivan Crossing Ski Resort and Lodge, we believe that life is better when you embrace the great outdoors. Nestled in the heart of the mountains, our resort offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation for all ages. Whether you're a seasoned skier or a first-time visitor, we have something for everyone.

Established in 1990, our resort has been a beloved destination for winter sports enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable experience that combines thrilling activities with warm hospitality and stunning natural beauty.

Where are we?

Located along the Cascade Range, we have what no business has to offer — untouched beauty, raw adventure, and a connection to nature that can't be bought or built. Plan your travel accordingly, because this is an experience that demands your full attention — and rewards it with unforgettable moments.
